Individual Project Site Plan

Site Name

G-NATH GLOBAL COMMUNICATIONS LTD

This name was selected because it is concise, memorable, and directly communicates the nature of the business, a physical and and online store specializing in phones and accessories. The LTD suffix signifies a retail outlet, reinforcing its purpose.

Site Purpose

The primary purpose of the G-NATH GLOBAL website is to establish a strong online presence for a small-scale business specializing in mobile phones, phone accessories, and various electronic gadgets. It aims to serve as a comprehensive platform where customers can easily browse, discover, and purchase products. The site will provide detailed product information, pricing, availability, and customer ratings, along with essential business contact information, fostering trust and accessibility for online shoppers. Ultimately, it seeks to expand the business's customer reach and facilitate online sales growth.

Scenarios

These questions represent typical inquiries from the target audience and will guide the content and functionality of the website:

  • "How can I quickly find a specific phone model, check its current price, and see if it's currently in stock?"
    (This drives the need for a robust product search/filter, clear product pages with pricing and availability, and potentially a 'popular products' section on the homepage.)
  • "I need to know the business's physical address, phone number, or email to get support for a recent purchase or ask a general inquiry."
    (This highlights the importance of a clear and comprehensive Contact Page with a functional form and all necessary contact details.)
  • "Are there any ongoing promotions or new arrivals for smartwatches or wireless headphones?"
    (This indicates the need for promotional banners on the homepage, a dynamic products page, and potentially categories for easy navigation.)

Color Schema

The following colors will be used to define the visual identity of the G-NATH GLOBAL COMMUNICATIONS website:

  • Primary Blue ($#007BFF$): This vibrant blue will be used for main headings, call-to-action buttons, interactive elements (like navigation links and modal triggers), and as an accent color for important information. It conveys trust, technology, and professionalism.
  • Dark Grey ($#333333$): This will be the primary color for all body text, paragraphs, and general content. It ensures excellent readability against light backgrounds.
  • Light Grey ($#F8F9FA$): This will serve as the default background color for the overall page and some section backgrounds, providing a clean, modern, and spacious feel, allowing product images and content to stand out.
  • Light Border Grey ($#DEE2E6$): Used for subtle borders, dividers, and outlines to visually separate sections and elements without being distracting.

Typography

The chosen fonts aim for a balance of modern aesthetics and high readability:

  • Montserrat (Sans-serif):
    Usage: Applied to all headings ($h1$, $h2$, $h3$, etc.). Its clean, geometric lines give a contemporary and authoritative feel, suitable for titles and important labels.
  • Open Sans (Sans-serif):
    Usage: Used for all body text, paragraphs, list items, and general content. Open Sans is highly legible across various screen sizes and provides a friendly yet professional appearance, ensuring comfortable reading for product descriptions and other information.
